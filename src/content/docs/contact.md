We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions about the API, feedback on the platform, or ideas for collaboration, don't hesitate to reach out.

## Email

The best way to reach us is by email:

📧 **[brittany@balancedengineer.com](mailto:brittany@balancedengineer.com)**

We typically respond within a few business days.

## What to Include

When reaching out, it helps if you include:

- **Bug reports** — Steps to reproduce, expected vs actual behavior, any error messages
- **Feature requests** — Description of the feature, your use case, and how it would help
- **API questions** — The endpoint you're working with, your request/response, and what you expected
- **Partnership inquiries** — Your project, how you'd like to integrate with Open Social, and your timeline

## Contributing

Open Social is open source. If you'd like to contribute:

- Check out our [GitHub organization](https://github.com/open-social) for repositories
- Open issues for bugs or feature requests
- Submit pull requests with improvements

We welcome contributions of all kinds — code, documentation, design, and feedback!
